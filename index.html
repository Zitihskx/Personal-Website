About

I grew up in the beautiful country of himalayas, Nepal. I am currently pursuing my dream as a PhD student in Computer Science Department of Tennessee Technological University, Tennessee, USA.

PhD Student & CyberSecurity Researcher.

As a graduate student researching at the fusion of machine learning and cybersecurity, I carry my expertise on applications of machine learning, data analytics and malware analysis. My current research focuses on the security of machine learning algorithms that are being used for malware detection. I am running experiments to carry out adversarial attacks on machine learning models in order to discover and patch vulnerabilities to make the models robust. My background have provided me with diverse experience which includes programming, embedded firmare development, robotics automation, running and customizing financial software, and monitoring databases.
